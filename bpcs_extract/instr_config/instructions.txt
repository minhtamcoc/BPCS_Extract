Lab-specific instructor notes.

This lab follows the native Labtainer grading format:
results.config defines the collected artifacts, and goals.config maps
those artifacts to cw1 through cw5.

Expected checks:
cw1: student saved ffmpeg video information to video_info.txt.
cw2: student extracted video frames to frames/frame_%04d.png.
cw3: student extracted the audio stream to audio.mp3.
cw4: student ran encode.py and created position.txt.
cw5: student ran decode.py and recovered the expected secret.
